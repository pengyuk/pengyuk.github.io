Evaluation of Synthetic Speech (Naturalness)

   The purpose of this study is to learn how to improve synthesised speech. In order to do this we are gathering people's opinions on the output of several different speech synthesis systems. You will be performing a series of evaluations of the speech produced by these systems. Please listen to the audio files below by clicking on the icons. The audio files will be presented to you in pairs. In each pair, the two audio files have the same content. Please listen to the two audio files, and then select which one sounds more natural or you have no preference by clicking the drop down list. You should not judge the grammar or content of the sentence, just how it sounds. Please listen with headphones.

    In order to complete the task, you had better use Chrome or Firefox browser. If you use Chrome, please allow for loading the script, following the method shown below.
